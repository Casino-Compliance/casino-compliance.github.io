Casino Compliance & Player Protection

Structured coordination of self-exclusion enforcement and dispute handling in online gambling environments.

What We Do

Self-Exclusion Coordination

Preparation, documentation and structured submission of self-exclusion notices, including proof of delivery and compliance tracking.

Refund & Compliance Claims

Coordination of complaints between players and operators relating to deposits, account handling, responsible gambling breaches and related issues.

Dispute Escalation

Structured escalation through internal operator channels and established third-party dispute and mediation platforms where applicable.

Casino Compliance is an independent claims and dispute coordination service. No legal advice, representation or regulatory authority is provided.
