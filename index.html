Casino Compliance & Player Protection

Structured coordination of self-exclusion enforcement and complaint handling between players and online gambling operators.
What We Do

Self-Exclusion Coordination

Preparation, documentation and structured transmission of self-exclusion notices, including preservation of delivery records.

Refund & Player Complaints

Coordination of complaints between players and operators relating to payments, account handling, responsible gambling obligations and procedures.

Dispute Escalation

Structured escalation through operator channels and established third-party dispute and mediation mechanisms where applicable.

Casino Compliance is an independent compliance and dispute coordination service. No legal advice, representation or regulatory authority is provided.
