Casino Compliance & Player Protection

Independent claims and dispute handling service for online gambling matters.

Casino Compliance provides structured claims handling and dispute coordination for players in relation to responsible gambling obligations, self-exclusion enforcement, and regulatory compliance.

Scope of Services

  • Self-Exclusion Enforcement Claims
  • Refund Claims Following Responsible Gambling Breaches
  • Dispute Coordination and Escalation
  • Evidence Review and Documentation

Working Principles

All matters are handled strictly on the basis of documented evidence. No legal advice or legal representation is provided. No regulatory authority is claimed.
