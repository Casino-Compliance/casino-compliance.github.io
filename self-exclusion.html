Self-Exclusion Enforcement

Self-exclusion is a mandatory responsible gambling measure requiring operators to prevent account access and financial transactions once a valid exclusion request has been submitted.

Casino Compliance provides structured coordination and documentation support for self-exclusion requests submitted to online gambling operators.

Purpose

The self-exclusion coordination process is designed to ensure that exclusion requests are transmitted in a verifiable manner and that subsequent operator conduct can be assessed against applicable responsible gambling obligations.

Process Overview

  1. Preparation of a standardized self-exclusion notice.
  2. Documented transmission to designated operator channels.
  3. Preservation of delivery confirmations.
  4. Monitoring of post-notice activity.

Self-Exclusion Coordination Tool

  • Centralized preparation of requests
  • Consistent formatting
  • Documented delivery records
  • Evidence preservation
